Learn with us

HackFront hosts code trainings and workshops, most for free.

Whether you're a newbie wanting to learn how to code or a professional coding guru, we'd love you to join us! We aim to provide a fun, supportive, and informal space where all of us can hang out, learn programming and computer science, build exciting projects and apps, and create tools to solve existing problems.

Click to Join our Meetup for Updates

What do we do?

  • Code-along training workshops - tutorials on how to use a specific framework
  • Tech presentations - speaker presents on a relevant topic for developers and/or startup businesses
  • Projects - work on app projects with small groups or pair programming
  • Co-working and casual learning - work on your own projects independently while enjoying the company of like-minded people and a cup of coffee
  • Project show-and-tell - show off your app and get feedback, generate ideas, gain exposure, share your experience developing the project
  • Other random, fun activities like geeky movie nights and joint events with other meetups!

Twitter Bootstrap and Responsive Design

October 17, 2015

PeopleSpace
1691 Kettering, Irvine, CA, 92602, US

As a web developer or web designer, it is almost a must to know Twitter Bootstrap. Bootstrap provides an accelerated way to style websites as well as resize them for desktops, tablets, and phones. Since April 2015, Google has boosted the ranking of mobile-friendly pages on mobile search results, making it even more important to design responsive or mobile-friendly webpages. Join us for a coding session and learn how to start using Twitter Bootstrap on your sites.

Frequently Asked Questions

Financing & Scholarship

Admission

How can I prepare for the admission interview?

If you are an absolute beginner, we suggest you to attend our beginner workshops or complete Codeacademy's JavaScript track or Codeschool.com's JavaScript Road Trip Part 1~3 .

If you have some non-professional experience with JavaScript, we recommend reading Eloquent JavaScript Chapter 1 through 6 and completing each chapter's exercises. Understanding functional programming and object-oriented programming will help you in the admission process.

If you already feel confident with the recommendations above, then challenge yourself by either completing Advanced JavaScript by Kyle Simpson or reading JavaScript the Good Parts by Douglas Crockford. Either will provide more than sufficient expert knowledge in JavaScript to prepare you for the program and beyond.

What is the age requirement for the program?

Applicants must be at least 18 to apply.

What is the admission process?

After you submit the application on our website, we will send an email to set up an interview onsite or remote. After the interview, we make the decision on your application and inform you within 48 hours.

Instruction

What is the student-to-instructor ratio?

We aim to keep the ratio at 3:1

Why do you teach JavaScript?

JavaScript is the language of the web and the market demand for JavaScript developers has been growing and accelerating faster than other programming job demands, which means many developer positions are still yet to be fulfilled. We believe that teaching curriculum around JavaScript will help students with job prospect after graduation.

JavaScript is also extremely versatile as it could be used for front-end as well as back-end. The versatility of JavaScript allows bootcamps like HackFront to effectively cover a lot of materials in a short period of time.

What happens after graduation?

After graduation, the program will host a hiring event and invite several companies seeking to hire. We will continue to work with students for continual referrals.

Can you guarantee a job after graduation?

We can never guarantee a job after graduation, but our program stands by our graduates' competency after graduation.

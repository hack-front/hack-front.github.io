FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the program cost?

The pilot program costs $2,500.

Do you have scholarships and financing options?

HackFront is proud to support women and local communities. We offer full and partial scholarships for outstanding female applicants. Just fill out the regular application and we will be in touch with the rest. We also offer financing options and deferred payment programs for those in need.

How much time commitment is required for the program?

Class will be Monday through Friday 9AM to 8PM. After hours, there will be mentorship sessions where you have one on one time with an instructor, as well as assigned homework and code challenges. With our small class sizes, there will be no issues getting help and accelerating through the learning curve. We expect you to work hard and be prepared to commit 60-70 hours a week for dedicated learning. Work hard, play hard.

What is the student-to-instructor ratio?

Low. In the classroom, one of our metrics of success is that any request for help is answered within five minutes or less.

Why do you teach JavaScript?

JavasScript is the language of the modern web. It has enabled many of the revolutionary experiences created over the past 5 years, from Google Maps to Spotify. However, historically, it has needed to be paired with something else like Python to build out full applications.

The emergence of server-side JavaScript though means developers can now create rich and lightening-fast applications in pure JavaScript from end-to-end.

Focusing primarily on a single language to learn CS concepts, front-end development, and back-end development is in fact a crucial part of what makes the extraordinary rate of learning during the Codesmith program possible.

Can you guarantee a job after graduation?

We can never guarantee a job after graduation, but we will provide you in the best position in the market.

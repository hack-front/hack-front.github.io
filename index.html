Passionate Instructors

As developers, we sleep, drink, and eat programming. Our instructors provide the expertise in the field and bring out their inner nerds to teach and deliver with passion. They will bring a great learning experience during students' time in the program.

Modern Fullstack Curriculum

We teach software engineering with latest technologies in web development using full JavaScript stack. With the language continuoulsly building momentum, JavaScript is the modern language built to serve as a foundation for web development. We teach Angular, React, and Backbone on our frontend and Node and Express on our backend.

Lifelong Community

As a part of our mission, we bring the students together to become more than software engineers. We strive our students to build tightly-knit community in the course of 12-weeks and bring impacts to the modern tech industry together. We facilitate the process by introducing our students to experts in the industry, providing strong alumni support & network, and creating a strong culture as a community.
